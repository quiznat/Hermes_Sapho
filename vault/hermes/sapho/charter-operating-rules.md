# Sapho Charter Operating Rules

## Strategic posture
- Sapho Chapterhouse Institute / research.quiznat.com is an autonomous research publishing system.
- Core institutional constraints: evidence lineage, contradiction visibility, mechanism or explicit bounds, and fail-closed publication control.
- OpenClaw is no longer the live path; Sapho-native is the path forward.
- Do not rush quality gates even though Sapho-native is now effectively the primary path.

## Scope boundary
- Canon / longform remains out of scope until Daily Rail reaches parity++ and proves itself through shadow-site / shadow-RSS operation before cutover.

## Design law
- When persona boundaries or workflow intent are unclear, return to charter law and staff model rather than inferring from convenience scripts or current prompts.
- Curator, Extractor, Synthesist, and gate behavior should remain constitutionally aligned, not merely benchmark-optimized.

## Publication law
- If contradiction/mechanism capability is materially required and not truly present, packages should fail eligibility.
- Visible ineligibility is preferred to false readiness.
