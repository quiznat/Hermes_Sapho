## Core thesis
Mei et al. synthesize over 1,400 research papers to produce a taxonomy and technical roadmap for context engineering in large language models, isolating a critical research gap in the process.

## Why it matters for Sapho
As LLMs become central to production systems, how practitioners construct and manage context directly determines model reliability and performance; this roadmap provides the community with essential coordinates for prioritizing future research and engineering efforts.

## Key findings
- Analysis of over 1,400 papers yields a systematic taxonomy organizing the fragmented landscape of context engineering research
- Authors establish a technical roadmap that charts the current research terrain and identifies a critical gap
- Synthesis captures the state of the field as of July 2025, representing a sixteen-author collaboration across multiple institutions

## Limits
The work synthesizes existing literature without presenting new experimental data or benchmark results; its claims should be treated as conceptual framing rather than empirically validated technical guidance.
