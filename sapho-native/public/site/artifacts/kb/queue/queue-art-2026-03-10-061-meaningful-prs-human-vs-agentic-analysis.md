## Thesis

Pull-request merge success in mixed human/agentic software workflows is strongly driven by submitter attributes, while review dynamics affect human and agentic PRs differently.

## Mechanism summary

The study performs large-scale regression analysis on 40,214 PRs from the AIDev dataset, extracting 64 features across six feature families to compare merge outcomes for human PRs, agentic PRs, and three AI agents.

## Why it matters for Sapho

This is direct empirical signal for software-factory governance: merge outcomes are not only model-quality effects, but also socio-technical effects tied to submitter identity and review process design, which implies policy/harness interventions may be as important as model changes.
