*   Context Engineering is introduced as a formal discipline for systematically optimizing information payloads for LLMs, extending beyond simple prompt design.
*   The survey categorizes Context Engineering into foundational Components (Retrieval/Generation, Processing, Management) and System Implementations (RAG, Memory Systems, Tool-Integrated Reasoning, Multi-Agent Systems).
*   A critical research gap is identified: LLMs excel at understanding complex contexts but are limited in generating equally sophisticated long-form outputs.
