# Persona and Job Model

## Definitions

- A `persona` is an enduring institute mind with a stable method, scope, and failure profile.
- A `job` is a bounded assignment given to a persona.
- A `gate` is a legal publication procedure.
- A `cadence` is when work happens.
- An `artifact` is the output contract of a job or gate.

## Daily rail rule

The Daily rail is modeled as:

- a small set of enduring personas
- a bounded set of repeatable jobs
- one gate that controls publication authority

The model must not create fake personas by naming jobs as if they were minds.

## Daily staff in scope now

- Curator
- Extractor
- Synthesist

Conclave is in scope as a gate, not as a persona.

Piter, heartbeat, and reflection are outside this Daily-staff refactor.

## Job ownership

- Curator owns the admission job.
- Extractor owns the evidence job.
- Synthesist owns the article-synthesis job.
- Synthesist owns the daily-synthesis job.
- Conclave owns the Daily publication gate.

## Charter discipline

Persona charters define:

- method
- strengths
- weaknesses
- boundaries
- refusal rules
- escalation rules

Job templates define:

- task objective
- inputs
- output contract
- forbidden moves
- acceptance shape

The persona charter is reusable across jobs.
The job template is replaceable without changing who the persona is.
